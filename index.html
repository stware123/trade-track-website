Running a Trade Business
Shouldn't Feel Like This

Most small contractors manage jobs across WhatsApp, paper timesheets, and memory. It works — until it doesn't.

📞

Constant phone calls

Workers ringing to ask where they're needed next. Managers spending hours a day just keeping people in the loop.

📋

Paper timesheets lost

Timesheets handed in late, illegible, or not at all. Payroll guesswork eats hours every week.

Jobs falling through gaps

No single source of truth means jobs get double-booked, forgotten, or started without all the details.

No visibility for management

Can't see who's on what job, what's running behind, or where the bottlenecks are — until a client rings angry.
